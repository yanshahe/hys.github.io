Welcome to Poképédia Green!

the environmental version of Pokémon encyclopedia

        Since its launch in April, 2022, Poképédia Green has strived to become 
        the bridge between the environmental philosophy in the Pokémon world and 
        the environmental issues in reality. We encourage every Pokémon fans to 
        perform as the real Pokémon rangers to protect our lovely world.

        We now have one crowdsourcing environmental project with over 100 rangers
        in Zooniverse and 22 Pokédex cards mapping the real world endangered animals. 
        If you want to be a Pokémon ranger, just join us! We need more rangers to 
        contribute to the environmental project and the website building!

Pokémon Rangers

Pokémon Rangers is a crowdsourcing environmental project in Zooniverse. We need you to help explore the environmental philosophy in the Pokémon world!
